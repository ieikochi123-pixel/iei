About the 39th National Convention

The Legacy-IE(I) Kochi Local Centre

The IE(I) Kochi Local Centre is a pivotal hub for engineering excellence in Kerala. We bridge the gap between academia and industry, serving as one of the most active centres for consultancy and professional development in the nation.

About National Convention

Coordinated by the Computer Engineering Division Board (CPDB), National Council, this convention follows the strategic vision of IE(I) to foster high-level technical discourse at a national scale.

Guidelines to Authors of Research Papers

AI & IoT for an Inclusive Society: Focused on using smart technologies as instruments of social equity, disability assistance, and barrier-free infrastructure for marginalized communities.

Benefits of Pulishing Research Papers

Integrating DRDO-inspired research, we explore Underwater IoT, submarine communication, and resilient AI-driven networks to ensure national security and coastal community safety.
